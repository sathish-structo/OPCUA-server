#include "ServerTripAlarm.h"

 

The TripAlarm is a specialization of the OffNormalAlarm intended to represent an equipment trip condition.

The Alarm becomes active when the monitored piece of equipment experiences some abnormal fault such as a motor shutting down due to an overload condition.
